2022 – 2023 Huawei ICT Competition Global Final 3rd Prize

I achieved a notable accomplishment in the field of Cloud Technologies in Turkey by ranking first in the Huawei ICT Competition 2022 - 2023, which involved over 120,000 students and more than 2,000 universities. After the national part is over, as the Turkey Cloud team, consisting of myself and two other teammates, we represented Turkey and Europe in the Global Final of the competition held in Shenzhen, China. Participating alongside 146 teams from various parts of the world.

Me and my team, attained a remarkable achievement by securing the 3rd prize in this global final.
