Privacy Policy

Application: Inventory Stock Manager Pro
Last Updated: December, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Inventory Stock Manager Pro (the “Application”) handles information in connection with your use of the Application. The Application is published on the Google Play Store by Deniz Özcan, under the publisher name GoodTimes Initiative (the “Developer”, “we”, “us”, or “our”).

The Application is designed with a strict offline-first and local-data-only architecture. Your data remains entirely under your control and is never accessible to the Developer.

1. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies solely to the Inventory Stock Manager Pro mobile application available on the Google Play Store. It does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications.

By installing or using the Application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection and Processing

2.1 No Data Collection

The Application does not collect, store, process, transmit, or access any personal or non-personal data.

  • No data is sent to the Developer.
  • No external servers are used.
  • No analytics, tracking, or profiling tools are implemented.
  • No device identifiers, IP addresses, or user accounts are collected.

All data created or entered by the user remains exclusively on the user’s device.

3. Local Data Storage

All inventory-related information, including but not limited to:

  • Product names and quantities
  • Barcodes
  • Product images
  • Categories and notes
  • Backup and restore files

is stored locally on the user’s device only. The Developer has no technical capability to access, view, retrieve, or recover this data.

4. App Permissions

The Application requests certain permissions solely to enable its core features. All permissions are used locally and never for data transmission.

4.1 Camera

Used exclusively for barcode scanning and capturing product images when explicitly initiated by the user.

4.2 File and Media Access

Used exclusively for:

  • Selecting product images
  • Creating local backups
  • Restoring backups from user-selected files

5. Internet Usage

The Application operates fully offline except for subscription-related functionality.

5.1 Google Play Billing

Internet access is required solely for subscription verification and purchase management via Google Play Billing.

All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. The Developer does not have access to payment details, billing information, or personally identifiable user data.

6. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google Play Billing. No advertising, analytics, tracking, or marketing SDKs are integrated into the Application.

7. Data Sharing

The Developer does not share data with third parties, as no user data is collected or accessible.

8. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally, data security depends on the user’s device, operating system, and personal security practices. The Developer cannot be held responsible for data loss caused by device failure, user error, or unauthorized access to the device.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13, or from users of any age, as no data collection occurs.

10. User Responsibility

The user is solely responsible for managing their data, maintaining backups, and securing access to their device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the Application or its Google Play Store listing. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
