Privacy Policy for FINITE: Life Perspective

Last Updated: December 2025

FINITE: Life Perspective (“the App”) is published on Google Play by GoodTimes Initiative and developed by Deniz Özcan (“I”, “me”, “my”). This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled. I am committed to transparency and protecting user privacy.

1. Overview

FINITE is an offline personal reflection and habit-building tool. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process personal data on external servers. All user data remains locally on the user's device.

2. Data Collection and Local Storage

The App does not:

  • collect personal information
  • store data on any external server
  • use analytics or tracking tools
  • access sensitive device permissions
  • share any user data with third parties

All user-generated content (journal entries, habits, reflections, tasks) is stored locally on the device and fully controlled by the user.

Data Deletion: Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data. I do not retain or have access to this data at any time.

3. Automatically Collected Information

The App itself does not collect analytics or device data. However, Google Play may provide anonymous, aggregated statistics such as:

  • install counts
  • device categories
  • country-level distribution

These reports contain no personally identifiable information and are generated solely by Google. For details, see Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional premium features through Google Play Billing (monthly subscription, yearly subscription, and lifetime unlock).

All transactions are processed by Google Play. I do not receive:

  • payment information
  • billing names or addresses
  • email addresses
  • Google account identifiers

Google Play may only notify the App of purchase status (e.g., “subscription active”), which is used locally to unlock features.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • advertising SDKs
  • analytics services
  • cloud databases
  • external servers

The only third-party service used is Google Play Billing, required for purchases.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. Since no data is collected, the App complies with relevant privacy protection standards.

7. Security

Because all information remains on the user's device, data security is dependent on the device's operating system and user practices. The App does not transmit data externally.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to ensure compliance with legal or platform requirements. The most current version will always be available on the listed URL.
