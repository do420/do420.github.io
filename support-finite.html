Frequently Asked Questions

What is FINITE?

FINITE is a simple app designed to help you become more aware of time and live more intentionally. It focuses on perspective, not productivity.

Do I need an account to use FINITE?

No. FINITE does not require any account or sign-up.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All data stays on your device. FINITE does not use cloud storage or external servers.

Does FINITE track or share my data?

No. FINITE does not track you, collect analytics, or share data with third parties.

How can I send feedback or report a bug?

You can send any feedback, ideas, or bug reports directly to makinggoodtimeshappen@gmail.com .
